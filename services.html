Our Comprehensive Services

At Gemmes et Bijoux, we offer a range of services to ensure your precious pieces are always at their best and tailored to your desires.

Custom Jewelry Design

Bring your unique vision to life with our bespoke design service. Our expert artisans work with you from concept to creation, crafting a piece that is truly one-of-a-kind and reflects your personal style.

Jewelry Repair & Restoration

Entrust your beloved jewelry to our skilled craftsmen for repair and restoration. From simple fixes to intricate overhauls, we carefully restore your pieces to their original beauty, preserving their sentimental and monetary value.

Watch Servicing & Repair

Our certified watchmakers provide comprehensive servicing and repair for all types of timepieces, from routine maintenance to complex mechanical repairs, ensuring precision and longevity for your valuable watches.

Appraisals & Valuations

We offer professional appraisal services for insurance purposes, estate planning, or personal knowledge. Our certified gemologists provide accurate and detailed valuations for your jewelry and watches.

Engraving & Personalization

Add a personal touch to your jewelry or timepiece with our engraving service. Commemorate special dates, names, or messages to make your gift truly unique and memorable.

Cleaning & Polishing

Keep your jewelry sparkling and new with our professional cleaning and polishing services. We use specialized techniques to safely remove dirt and restore brilliance without compromising your precious stones or metals.
