About Gemmes et Bijoux

For over two decades, Gemmes et Bijoux has been a beacon of luxury and craftsmanship. We specialize in creating bespoke jewelry pieces, from dazzling engagement rings to intricate heirloom restorations. Our passion lies in sourcing the finest ethically-mined gems and transforming them into wearable art that celebrates life's most precious moments.
